What you told us

Five themes from our discovery conversations — in your words, not ours. Today's job is to map each one to a concrete RCA answer.

Pain 1

Legacy Tech Debt

Org built 2014–2016. Heavy Apex, Visualforce, workflow rules, process builders. New Salesforce features can't be adopted.

Cost: every release passes Britten by.

Pain 2

Scaling Pricing

Only Print has the Apex Estimator. Foam3D™, BoxPop®, Woodworking run on "sophisticated spreadsheets" with no path into Salesforce.

Cost: 3 of 4 divisions can't scale on the platform.

Pain 3

Inflexible Catalog

Adding new capabilities (e.g. fabric printing) is "clunky" and "a bear to update." Each change is a multi-week dev cycle.

Cost: growth is gated by IT capacity.

Pain 4

Two-Person Team

Anthony & Colin maintain the entire org. Explicit preference for "admin-friendly" config. Past partner experience was scrapped.

Cost: all change requests funnel through 1.5 FTE.

Pain 5

Decomposition

One quote line = 100 art files or multi-state shipments. Today managed via custom junction objects sitting between Job_Item__c and Invoice.

Cost: brittle glue layer that breaks on edge cases.

Before we go anywhere near RCA today, I want to read back what we heard from you. Five themes, all in your words. If any of these are wrong or have shifted since we last talked, please stop me. The whole point of today is to make sure we're solving the actual problem — not the one we imagined.
